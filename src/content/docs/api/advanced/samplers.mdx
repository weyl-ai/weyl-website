Samplers determine how the model iteratively denoises from random noise to your final image/video.

## Available Samplers

| Sampler   | Speed | Quality | Stability | Best For                  ||-----------|-------|---------|-----------|---------------------------|| `euler`   | Fast  | Good    | High      | General purpose, default  || `uni_pc`  | Fast  | Good    | High      | WAN video, fast iteration || `res_2m`  | Medium| Better  | High      | Quality, RES4LYF          || `res_3s`  | Slow  | Best    | High      | Maximum quality           |

## Euler**Default for most models**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "sampler": "euler"}

```**Characteristics:**

- Fast convergence

- Stable across step counts

- General purpose

- Works well with all schedulers**Best for:**

- Default choice

- Fast iteration

- Predictable results

## UniPC**Default for WAN**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "sampler": "uni_pc"}

```**Characteristics:**

- Predictor-corrector method

- Fast convergence

- Excellent for video

- Lower step counts (10-20)**Best for:**

- WAN video generation

- Fast iteration

- Lower step budgets

## RES 2M (RES4LYF)**Recommended for quality**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "sampler": "res_2m",  "steps": 30}

```**Characteristics:**

- Residual feedback

- Better detail than euler

- 2-moment formulation

- Requires more steps (25-40)**Best for:**

- High-quality final renders

- Detail preservation

- FLUX Dev/Dev2

- WAN with quality focus**Performance impact:*

* ~10% slower than euler at same step count

## RES 3S**Maximum quality**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "sampler": "res_3s",  "steps": 40}

```**Characteristics:**

- 3rd-order solver

- Best quality

- Highest stability

- Requires higher step counts (30-50)**Best for:**

- Absolute maximum quality

- Final production renders

- When speed not critical**Performance impact:*

* ~20% slower than euler

## Visual Comparison#

## Euler vs RES 2M**Euler (25 steps):**

- Clean output

- Good details

- Slight smoothing in high-frequency areas**RES 2M (30 steps):**

- Sharper details

- Better texture preservation

- Slightly longer generation timeDifference is subtle but noticeable in detailed scenes (faces, textures, fine patterns).#

## UniPC vs RES 2M (WAN Video)**UniPC (20 steps):**

- Fast convergence

- Good motion

- Smooth temporal consistency**RES 2M (25 steps):**

- Better frame detail

- Improved texture retention

- Slightly better motion qualityFor video, difference is minimal unless generating high-detail scenes.

## Error Tolerance#

## Stability Ranking
1. **res_3s*

* - Most stable, highest error tolerance
2. **res_2m*

* - Very stable, good error handling
3. **euler*

* - Stable, occasional artifacts at low steps
4. **uni_pc*

* - Stable for intended use cases (video)#

## Low Step PerformanceSome samplers degrade faster at low step counts:**10 steps:**

- euler: Usable but noisy

- uni_pc: Good for video

- res_2m: Not recommended

- res_3s: Not recommended**15 steps:**

- euler: Good

- uni_pc: Excellent

- res_2m: Acceptable

- res_3s: Not recommended**25+ steps:**

- All samplers perform well

## Advanced Usage#

## Sampler SwitchingSome models benefit from starting with one sampler and switching mid-generation:

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "sampler": "euler",  "sampler_switch": {    "at_step": 15,    "to": "res_2m"  },  "steps": 30}

```Fast convergence with euler, then fine detail with res_2m. (Coming soon)#

## Custom Step SchedulesOverride per-step behavior (advanced):

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "sampler": "res_2m",  "sampler_config": {    "momentum": 0.9,    "order": 2  }}

```(Coming soon 

- contact support for custom configurations)

## Choosing a Sampler#

## Decision Tree

```Need video generation?└─ Yes → uni_pc (default) or res_2m (quality)└─ No → Image generation    └─ Priority: Speed?        └─ Yes → euler        └─ No → Priority: Quality?            └─ Yes → res_3s            └─ No → res_2m (balanced)

```#

## By Use Case

| Use Case                  | Recommended Sampler ||---------------------------|---------------------|| Fast iteration            | euler               || Final renders (image)     | res_2m or res_3s    || Video generation          | uni_pc              || High-detail video         | res_2m              || Production quality        | res_3s              || Balanced (default)        | euler               |
