Schedulers control the noise reduction schedule during generation. They determine how quickly the model transitions from random noise to your final image.

## Available Schedulers

| Scheduler      | Speed | Quality | Best For                     ||----------------|-------|---------|------------------------------|| `simple`       | Fast  | Good    | Fast iteration, previews     || `beta`         | Fast  | Good    | General purpose, default     || `exponential`  | Medium| Better  | Smooth gradients, quality    || `sgm_uniform`  | Medium| Good    | SDM-style generation         || `bong_tangent` | Slow  | Best    | WAN video, bidirectional     |

## Simple**Fastest scheduler**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "scheduler": "simple"}

```**Characteristics:**

- Linear noise schedule

- Fast computation

- Minimal overhead

- Good for iteration**Best for:**

- Rapid prototyping

- Fast preview generation

- Development/testing

- Low step counts

## Beta**Default for most models**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "scheduler": "beta"}

```**Characteristics:**

- Beta distribution schedule

- Balanced performance

- Stable across step counts

- General purpose**Best for:**

- Default choice

- Most use cases

- FLUX models

- Balanced quality/speed

## Exponential**Smooth noise reduction**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "scheduler": "exponential",  "steps": 30}

```**Characteristics:**

- Exponential decay schedule

- Smoother gradients

- Better detail preservation

- Slightly slower**Best for:**

- High-quality renders

- Smooth color gradients

- Photorealistic images

- FLUX Dev/Dev2**Performance impact:*

* ~5% slower than beta

## SGM Uniform**Stable Diffusion-style schedule**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "scheduler": "sgm_uniform"}

```**Characteristics:**

- Uniform noise schedule

- SDM-compatible

- Consistent with SD workflows

- Good compatibility**Best for:**

- Users familiar with SD

- Replicating SD results

- Compatibility workflows

## Bong Tangent**Bidirectional scheduler for video**

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "scheduler": "bong_tangent",  "steps": 25}

```**Characteristics:**

- Bidirectional attention

- Optimized for video

- Best temporal consistency

- Requires shift parameter for WAN**Best for:**

- **WAN video generation (recommended)**

- Temporal coherence

- High-quality video**Requirements:**

- Works best with `sampler: res_2m`

- WAN models require `shift: 5` for optimal results

## Step Count Impact#

## Minimum Steps by Scheduler

| Scheduler      | Min Steps | Optimal Steps ||----------------|-----------|---------------|| simple         | 4         | 15            || beta           | 10        | 25            || exponential    | 15        | 30            || sgm_uniform    | 15        | 25            || bong_tangent   | 20        | 25            |Lower step counts may cause artifacts or incomplete convergence.

```**Balanced Quality:**

```json{  "sampler": "euler",  "scheduler": "beta",  "steps": 25}

```**High Quality (Image):**

```json{  "sampler": "res_2m",  "scheduler": "exponential",  "steps": 30}

```**Best Quality (Video):**

```json{  "sampler": "res_2m",  "scheduler": "bong_tangent",  "steps": 25}

```

## Visual Comparison#

## Beta vs Exponential**Beta (25 steps):**

- Standard noise reduction

- Good balance

- Slight stepping in gradients**Exponential (30 steps):**

- Smoother color transitions

- Better gradient quality

- More natural lightingDifference is subtle but visible in sky gradients, smooth surfaces, and lighting transitions.#

## Simple vs Bong Tangent (WAN)**Simple (20 steps):**

- Fast generation

- Good temporal consistency

- Occasional temporal artifacts**Bong Tangent (25 steps):**

- Better frame coherence

- Smoother motion

- More natural animationCritical for video quality.

```json{  "scheduler": "bong_tangent",  "shift": 5  // Default for WAN, 0-10 range}

```Higher shift = more temporal smoothing, less motion freedom.

## Common Issues#

## Artifacts at Low Steps**Symptom:*

* Noise/artifacts in output at low step counts**Solution:**

- Increase steps to scheduler minimum

- Use simpler scheduler (beta or simple)

- Try different sampler#

## Color Banding**Symptom:*

* Visible steps in smooth gradients**Solution:**

- Use `exponential` scheduler

- Increase step count

- Try `res_2m` sampler#

## Temporal Artifacts (Video)**Symptom:*

* Frame flickering or inconsistency**Solution:**

- Use `bong_tangent` scheduler

- Set `shift: 5`

- Increase steps to 25+
