The detail enhancement daemon provides fine-grained control over detail preservation and enhancement during generation.

## OverviewDetail enhancement works by applying differential feedback during specific portions of the generation process, preserving or enhancing high-frequency content.

## Basic Usage

```json{  "prompt": "macro photography of flower petals",  "detail_amount": 0.5,  "detail_start": 0.2,  "detail_end": 0.8}

```

## Parameters#

## detail_amount**Range:*

* 0.0 to 1.0  **Default:*

* 0.0 (disabled)  **Recommended:*

* 0.3 to 0.7Strength of detail enhancement:

| Value | Effect                          ||-------|---------------------------------|| 0.0   | Disabled (no enhancement)       || 0.3   | Subtle detail boost             || 0.5   | Moderate enhancement            || 0.7   | Strong detail preservation      || 1.0   | Maximum (may cause artifacts)   |#

## detail_start**Range:*

* 0.0 to 1.0  **Default:*

* 0.0  **Recommended:*

* 0.1 to 0.3When to start enhancing (normalized step progress):

- `0.0` 

- From first step

- `0.2` 

- After 20% of steps

- `0.5` 

- Halfway through**Best practice:*

* Start after initial structure forms (`0.2`)#

## detail_end**Range:*

* 0.0 to 1.0  **Default:*

* 1.0  **Recommended:*

* 0.7 to 0.9When to stop enhancing:

- `0.8` 

- Stop at 80% progress

- `1.0` 

- Until last step**Best practice:*

* Stop before final steps to avoid over-sharpening (`0.8`)

```Preserves fine textures and patterns.#

## Troubleshooting#

## Over-Sharpening**Symptom:*

* Unnatural sharpness, halos, artifacts**Solution:**

- Lower `detail_amount` (try 0.3 instead of 0.7)

- End earlier (`detail_end: 0.7`)

- Reduce step count slightly#

## No Visible Effect**Symptom:*

* Detail enhancement not apparent**Solution:**

- Increase `detail_amount` (try 0.6)

- Extend range (start: 0.1, end: 0.9)

- Increase total steps (30+)

- Ensure prompt mentions detail/texture#

## Artifacts**Symptom:*

* Strange patterns, noise**Solution:**

- Start later (`detail_start: 0.3`)

- End earlier (`detail_end: 0.7`)

- Lower amount

- Use better sampler (res_2m)

## Best Practices
1. **Start conservative*

* - Begin with amount 0.3-0.5
2. **Match to subject*

* - Portraits need less than product shots
3. **Don't enhance entire generation*

* - Use start/end windows
4. **Combine with right sampler*

* - res_2m works best
5. **Increase steps*

* - Detail needs more steps (28-35)
6. **Test systematically*

* - Change one parameter at a time
