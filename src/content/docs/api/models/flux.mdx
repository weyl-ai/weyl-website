## Model Variants#

## FLUX.2 Dev (dev2)**32B parameter model with Mistral-3 24B text encoder**

```bashcurl -X POST "https://sync.render.weyl.ai/image/flux/dev2/t2i?format=1024" \  -H "Authorization: Bearer $WEYL_API_KEY" \  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \  -d '{    "prompt": "portrait of a person in natural light, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field",    "guidance": 3.5,    "steps": 25  }' \  -o output.webp

```**Characteristics:**

- Best prompt understanding

- Excellent fine detail

- Great coherence on complex scenes

- ~2.4s @ 1024px on nunchaku**Guidance Tuning:**

- Short prompts: `guidance: 3.5`

- Detailed prompts: `guidance: 1.5`

- Very detailed: `guidance: 1.0`#

## FLUX.1 Dev (dev)**12B parameter model with T5-XXL encoder**

```bashcurl -X POST "https://sync.render.weyl.ai/image/flux/dev/t2i?format=landscape" \  -H "Authorization: Bearer $WEYL_API_KEY" \  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \  -d '{    "prompt": "mountain landscape at golden hour, dramatic clouds",    "guidance": 3.5,    "steps": 25  }' \  -o output.webp

```**Characteristics:**

- Excellent quality/speed balance

- Strong baseline model

- Wide LoRA ecosystem

- ~1.8s @ 1024px on nunchaku**Guidance:**

- Standard: `guidance: 3.5`

- Photorealism: `guidance: 2.8`#

## FLUX.1 Schnell (schnell)**12B distilled model, optimized for 4 steps**

```bashcurl -X POST "https://sync.render.weyl.ai/image/flux/schnell/t2i?format=512" \  -H "Authorization: Bearer $WEYL_API_KEY" \  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \  -d '{    "prompt": "cyberpunk alley at night, neon signs",    "steps": 4  }' \  -o output.webp

```**Characteristics:**

- Fastest FLUX variant

- Fixed 4 steps

- Good quality for speed

- ~450ms @ 1024px on nunchaku

- Apache-2.0 license**Fixed Parameters:**

- Steps: `4` (always)

- Guidance: `3.5` (fixed)

## Tasks#

## Text-to-Image (t2i)All FLUX models support t2i:

```json{  "prompt": "portrait of a character, cinematic lighting",  "negative_prompt": "blur, distortion, low quality",  "steps": 25,  "guidance": 3.5,  "seed": 42}

```#

## Image-to-Image (i2i)Transform existing images:

```json{  "prompt": "convert to watercolor painting style",  "image": "https://example.com/photo.jpg",  "strength": 0.65,  "guidance": 3.5}

```**Strength guide:**

- `0.1-0.3` 

- Subtle adjustments

- `0.4-0.6` 

- Balanced transformation

- `0.7-0.9` 

- Heavy changes

## Advanced Parameters#

## Samplers

| Sampler   | Notes                              | Best For        ||-----------|------------------------------------|-----------------|| `euler`   | Default, fast and stable           | General purpose || `res_2m`  | RES4LYF, better detail than euler  | Quality         || `res_3s`  | Best quality, slower               | Final renders   |

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "sampler": "res_2m",  "steps": 30}

```#

## Schedulers

| Scheduler      | Notes                          ||----------------|--------------------------------|| `simple`       | Fast iteration                 || `beta`         | Good default                   || `exponential`  | Smoother gradients             || `sgm_uniform`  | SDM-style schedule             |

```json{  "prompt": "...",  "scheduler": "exponential"}

```#

## LoRAsApply LoRA adapters for enhanced results:

```json{  "prompt": "portrait with natural lighting",  "lora": [    {      "id": "anti_blur",      "weight": 0.8    },    {      "id": "realism_boost",      "weight": 0.9    }  ]}

```See [LoRA documentation](/api/advanced/loras/) for available adapters.#

## Detail EnhancementFine-grained detail control:

```json{  "prompt": "macro photography of flower",  "detail_amount": 0.5,  "detail_start": 0.2,  "detail_end": 0.8}

```

| Backend    | Dev2 | Dev | Schnell | Notes                     ||------------|------|-----|---------|---------------------------|| `nunchaku` | ✓    | ✓   | ✓       | Default, FP4, fastest     || `torch`    | ✓    | ✓   | ✓       | Full precision, flexible  || `tensorrt` | 

-    

| ✓   | ✓       | TRT-optimized, production |Override with `?backend=`:

```bashcurl -X POST "https://sync.render.weyl.ai/image/flux/dev/t2i?format=1024&backend=tensorrt" \  ...

```

## Quality Tips#

## For Portraits

```json{  "prompt": "portrait of a person, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field, natural lighting, film grain",  "model": "dev2",  "guidance": 3.5,  "steps": 28,  "lora": [    {"id": "anti_blur", "weight": 0.8},    {"id": "realism_boost", "weight": 0.75}  ]}

```#

## For Photorealism

```json{  "prompt": "photograph of [subject], natural lighting, high detail, sharp focus",  "model": "dev",  "guidance": 2.8,  "steps": 30,  "sampler": "res_2m",  "scheduler": "exponential"}

```#

## For Speed

```json{  "prompt": "[description]",  "model": "schnell",  "format": "512",  "steps": 4}

```#

## For Style Transfer

```json{  "prompt": "in the style of [artist/style], [additional details]",  "image": "https://example.com/source.jpg",  "strength": 0.65,  "model": "dev",  "guidance": 3.5}

```

## Prompt Engineering#

## Structure

```[subject], [style/medium], [lighting], [mood], [technical details]

```**Example:**

```portrait of a woman, oil painting, golden hour lighting, serene mood, high detail, bokeh background

```#

## Negative PromptsEffective negative prompts for FLUX:

```blur, distortion, artifacts, low quality, bad anatomy, deformed, cropped, out of frame

```#

## Guidance by Prompt Length

| Prompt Length | dev2 Guidance | dev Guidance ||---------------|---------------|--------------|| 1-10 words    | 3.5           | 3.5          || 10-30 words   | 2.5           | 3.5          || 30-50 words   | 1.5           | 3.0          || 50+ words     | 1.0           | 2.8          |
