Weyl supports multiple model families, each optimized for different use cases.

## Active Models

| Family   | Modality | Models           | Status       | Best For                    ||----------|----------|------------------|--------------|------------------------------|| `flux`   | image    | dev2, dev, schnell | ✓ Active    | General purpose, quality     || `zimage` | image    | turbo            | ✓ Active    | Speed, iteration             || `wan`    | video    | default          | Coming Soon | Image animation              |

## Model Selection Guide#

## FLUX Family**Black Forest Labs 

- State of the art image generation**

- **dev2*

* - FLUX.2 Dev (32B parameters)  

- Best quality, latest architecture  

- Mistral-3 24B text encoder  

- Excellent prompt adherence  

- ~2.4s @ 1024px on nunchaku

- **dev*

* - FLUX.1 Dev (12B parameters)  

- Strong baseline, T5-XXL encoder  

- Great balance of quality and speed  

- ~1.8s @ 1024px on nunchaku

- **schnell*

* - FLUX.1 Schnell (12B parameters, distilled)  

- Fastest, 4-step generation  

- Good quality for most use cases  

- ~450ms @ 1024px on nunchaku#

## Z-Image Family**Alibaba Tongyi Lab 

- Ultra-fast generation**

- **turbo*

* - Z-Image Turbo (6B parameters)  

- Sub-second at 1024px (~320ms)  

- Apache-2.0 license  

- Great for iteration, prototyping  

- Fixed CFG 1.0#

## WAN Family (Coming Soon)**Video generation from images**

- **default*

* - WAN 2.2 (MoE architecture)  

- Animate still images  

- 0.5-10 second clips  

- 720p and 480p output  

- Excellent motion coherence

## Format Support#

## Image FormatsAll image models support:

| Format           | Dimensions | Aspect ||------------------|------------|--------|| `1024`           | 1024×1024  | 1:1    || `512`            | 512×512    | 1:1    || `portrait`       | 768×1024   | 3:4    || `portrait-wide`  | 576×1024   | 9:16   || `landscape`      | 1024×768   | 4:3    || `landscape-wide` | 1024×576   | 16:9   |#

## Choosing a Model#

## For Speed
1. **zimage/turbo*

* - Sub-second (320ms)
2. **flux/schnell*

* - Very fast (450ms)
3. **flux/dev*

* - Fast (1.8s)#

## For Quality
1. **flux/dev2*

* - Best quality, latest
2. **flux/dev*

* - Excellent baseline
3. **flux/schnell*

* - Good for 4 steps#

## For Iteration

- **zimage/turbo*

* - Rapid prototyping

- **flux/schnell*

* - Fast preview generation

- **flux/dev*

* - Final renders#

## For Video

- **wan/default*

* - Only option currently (coming soon)
