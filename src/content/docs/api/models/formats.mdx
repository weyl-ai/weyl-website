Complete reference for video and image output formats.

## Image FormatsAll image models support these formats via `?format=` parameter.#

## Square Formats##

## 1024

```Dimensions: 1024×1024Aspect: 1:1Use: High-resolution square

```**Best for:*

* Profile pictures, Instagram posts, product shots**Performance:**

- FLUX Schnell: ~450ms

- FLUX Dev: ~1.8s

- Z-Image Turbo: ~320ms##

## 512

```Dimensions: 512×512Aspect: 1:1Use: Fast iteration, thumbnails

```**Best for:*

* Rapid prototyping, previews, icons**Performance:**

- FLUX Schnell: ~180ms

- FLUX Dev: ~680ms

- Z-Image Turbo: ~140ms#

## Portrait Formats##

## portrait

```Dimensions: 768×1024Aspect: 3:4Use: Standard portrait

```**Best for:*

* Portrait photos, character art##

## portrait-wide

```Dimensions: 576×1024Aspect: 9:16Use: Vertical video aspect

```**Best for:*

* TikTok, Instagram Stories, phone displays#

## Landscape Formats##

## landscape

```Dimensions: 1024×768Aspect: 4:3Use: Standard landscape

```**Best for:*

* Landscape photos, artwork##

## landscape-wide

```Dimensions: 1024×576Aspect: 16:9Use: Widescreen

```**Best for:*

* YouTube thumbnails, desktop wallpapers, cinematic

## Video FormatsVideo models (WAN) support these formats.#

## Landscape Formats##

## 720p

```Dimensions: 1280×720Aspect: 16:9FPS: 24Use: HD landscape video

```**Best for:*

* YouTube, Vimeo, desktop viewing**Performance (WAN, 2s):*

* ~12s##

## 480p

```Dimensions: 832×480Aspect: ~16:9FPS: 24Use: SD landscape, faster generation

```**Best for:*

* Iteration, previews, faster workflows**Performance (WAN, 2s):*

* ~8s#

## Portrait Formats##

## 720p-portrait

```Dimensions: 720×1280Aspect: 9:16FPS: 24Use: HD portrait video

```**Best for:*

* TikTok, Instagram Reels, mobile**Performance (WAN, 2s):*

* ~12s##

## 480p-portrait

```Dimensions: 480×832Aspect: ~9:16FPS: 24Use: SD portrait, faster generation

```**Best for:*

* Fast iteration, mobile previews**Performance (WAN, 2s):*

* ~8s#

## Square Format##

## square

```Dimensions: 640×640Aspect: 1:1FPS: 24Use: Square social video

```**Best for:*

* Instagram posts (1:1), social media**Performance (WAN, 2s):*

* ~10s

## Format Selection Guide#

## By Use Case

| Use Case              | Recommended Format      ||-----------------------|-------------------------|| Profile picture       | `1024` (image)          || Instagram post        | `1024` (image)          || TikTok                | `720p-portrait` (video) || YouTube               | `720p` (video)          || Portrait photo        | `portrait` (image)      || Landscape photo       | `landscape` (image)     || Wallpaper             | `landscape-wide` (image)|| Fast iteration        | `512` (image)           || Character concept     | `portrait` (image)      || Cinematic frame       | `landscape-wide` (image)|#

## By Platform

| Platform              | Format                  ||-----------------------|-------------------------|| Instagram Post        | `1024` (1:1)            || Instagram Story       | `portrait-wide` (9:16)  || TikTok                | `720p-portrait` (9:16)  || YouTube               | `720p` or `landscape-wide` (16:9) || Twitter               | `landscape` (4:3) or `landscape-wide` (16:9) || LinkedIn              | `landscape` (4:3)       || Facebook              | `1024` (1:1) or `landscape` |#

## By Speed Requirements**Fastest to Slowest (Z-Image Turbo):**
1. `512` 

- ~140ms
2. `landscape-wide` 

- ~220ms
3. `portrait-wide` 

- ~230ms
4. `portrait` 

- ~250ms
5. `landscape` 

- ~250ms
6. `1024` 

- ~320ms**General rule:*

* Smaller resolution = faster generation

## Output Specifications#

## Image Output

- **Format:*

* WebP

- **Quality:*

* 90 (configurable)

- **Color Space:*

* sRGB

- **Bit Depth:*

* 8-bit

- **Compression:*

* Lossy WebP#

## Video Output

- **Container:*

* MP4

- **Codec:*

* H.264

- **Profile:*

* High

- **Bitrate:*

* ~8 Mbps (720p), ~4 Mbps (480p)

- **FPS:*

* 24

- **Color Space:*

* BT.709

- **Audio:*

* None (silent)

## Custom FormatsCustom dimensions not currently supported. Use closest standard format and crop/resize client-side if needed.

## CDN URLsAll outputs are served from `cdn.render.weyl.ai` with cache-forever semantics:

```https://cdn.render.weyl.ai/i/abc123.webp  # imagehttps://cdn.render.weyl.ai/v/xyz789.mp4   # video

```
